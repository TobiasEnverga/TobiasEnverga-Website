Tobias Enverga

Biology Major

Born May 26, 2001 and always wanted to pursue something great.

Being the third Tobias, I always had big shoes to fill, for the first was a World War II veteran and successful writer in the Phillippines, while the second was the first filipino senator of canada, and honestly, I did not know what I wanted to be. I thought at first I wanted to be the president of the United States, but that dream was short lived. It wasn't until 6th grade that I wanted to go and study monkeys as a zooologist which was inspired by my growing fondness of learning about the early Humans. When I got into middle school, I had to think more realistically and finallY, I decided on being an Optometrist.

That dream of mine still holds to this day and I worked hard to be at where I am today. I studied hard to be on track to graduating early, and I set up a whole plan for me to successfully be accepted into and graduate Optometry School. I want to live out my life with my family in a small city and pass on the name Tobias Enverga to my next born son.
